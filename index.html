Some Thoughts About Fly Fishing

Thanks for visiting! I'd like to tell you some things about fly fishing.

"A bad day fishing is better than a good day at work"

If you've never been fishing, you may think that the most important aspect is to catch a fish. "Did you catch anything?" is always the first question asked by those who aren't familiar with this idea. Sure, catching fish is exciting, and in a time where supermarkets didn't exist, that was understandably the priority.

With fly fishing, the number one objective is simply to be fly fishing. Catching fish is secondary, and is definitely not required. Does the thrill of a fish striking your fly improve the experience? Without a doubt. But there is a good chance you won't experience this thrill very often when you first begin. What makes this fun is standing in a river, feeling the movement of the world around you, and possibly enjoying an up beverage, if that's your bag.
